How Transformers Understand Context

The core of a transformer is the "Attention Mechanism." Instead of just reading words in order, it weighs the importance of every word in relation to every other word to grasp the true meaning, much like a human does intuitively.

"The mechanic tightened the bolt, but it was too rusty."

⬇

The AI asks: "What does 'it' refer to?"

Mechanic? (Low Attention)
Bolt? (High Attention)
Tightened? (Low Attention)
⬇

Conclusion: "It" is the bolt.

The model assigns a higher "attention score" to "bolt," correctly identifying the subject and its state ("rusty").
