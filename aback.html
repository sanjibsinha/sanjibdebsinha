The Trust Paradox

Why Our Relationship with AI Will Be... Complicated.

Are We Ready for an AI Partner?

As we crave escapes from the complexities of human relationships, the allure of a logical, predictable AI companion grows. But this desire sets the stage for a fundamental conflict between our nature and the machine's programming. Surveys suggest a significant portion of the population is already open to the idea, highlighting the urgency of understanding this trust dynamic.

Anatomy of a Human Lie

Humans lie for a multitude of complex social and psychological reasons, from sparing feelings to outright self-preservation. This behavior, deeply ingrained in our species, is a minefield for a logical AI. What we see as nuance, a machine may only process as a data error, creating a foundational misunderstanding in how we communicate.
